+ How to Play +

You are trying to guess a word of no less than three and no more than nine letters. Your guesses will be given a percentage score based on how many letters they have in common with the answer, and how close those letters are to the correct positions.

It will likely take you several dozen guesses to find the solution. To help, the letters for any guess that scores zero percent will be grayed out on the keyboard.
