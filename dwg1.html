+ How to Play +

Use letters from the grid to form words of at least two letters. Each letter can only be used once, except for the center square which will be available for every word.

Words score points based on their lengths.

Word Length 12345 678910 1112131415
Score 00012 357912 1518222630

The score for each round will be your longest word multiplied by your other words added together, with a bonus for the minimum word length.

If you clear the board (not including the center square) it will reset and the minimum word length (indicated by undescores) will increase. Scores from each round will be added together.

You can view your score summary any time by clicking on your score. This screen will appear automatically if the game thinks you're stuck. Good luck!
