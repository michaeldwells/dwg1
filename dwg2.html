+ How to Play +

Combine the blocks to find the longest possible word.

You may guess as often as you like.

If part of your guess is in the solution, those blocks will be combined.

+ Notes +

Sometimes it seems too easy. Often I find it nearly impossible.

I'm considering a light scoring mechanism whereby you're punished for guesses that aren't in the dictionary, and possibly a limit to the number of guesses allowed.
