+ How to Play +

Use letters from the grid to form words. Each letter can only be used once, except for the center square which will be available for every word.

Words score points based on their lengths. The first three letters of each word score zero. The next three letters score one point each. Then two points each for the next three, and so on.

The score for the round will be your longest word multiplied by your other words added together.

If you clear the board, it will reset, and the minimum word length (indicated by undescores) will go up by one. Scores from each round will be added together.

Good luck!
