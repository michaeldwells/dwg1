+ How to Play +

Select a tag from the bottom, and slide it into one of the rows or columns of the puzzle. You are trying to form six six-letter words.

Tapping a tag in the puzzle will push it one letter accross. When a tag is all the way accross, tapping on the far side will remove it from the puzzle and return it to the wall. Tags inserted from the right or bottom will be flipped.
