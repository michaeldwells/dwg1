+ How to Play +

Combine the blocks to find the longest possible word.

You may guess as often as you like, but each guess must be in the dictionary.

To help you, if any part of your guess is in the solution, the matching blocks will be permanently combined.

+ Notes +

The dictionary for validating guesses is much bigger than the one used to generate the puzzle, so it is possible that a longer word than the solution will be accepted as a guess.

There is currently no mechanism for giving up and finding out what the answer was.
