+ How to Play +

You are trying to guess a word of seven to twelve letters. Your guesses will be given a percentage score and a symbol.

---There is a run of 3 letters in the guess that are not in the answer, and no run of shared letters that is as long as this.
-7There is a run of 7 letters in the guess that are not in the answer, and no run of shared letters that is as long as this.
++++There is a run of 4 letters in the guess that are also in the answer, and no run of different letters that is as long as this.
**All letters in the guess are also in the answer. The longest sequence in the guess that is also in the answer is 2 letters long.
